This mod contains random jokers I come up with.

New jokers will be added every once and a while!

JOKERS (As of 3/6/25)

Breaking News

3X mult if played hand hasn't been played before this round.

Well-Used Joker

Gains $7 of sell value every round.
1 in 3 chance joker is destroyed at end of round.

Viral Joker

Gains +2 for every enhanced Heart card scored.

The Third Dimension

Gains +33 chips for
every held 3 in hand.

Retexture

Every face card held in hand gives +30 chips.

Mish-mash

After 5 rounds,
sell this joker for a 1 in 3 chance to duplicate the joker to the left.

Crash Log

Gains X0.5 mult every time a joker is destroyed.

Solid Gold

Retrigger all Gold Seals.

Peekaboo!

1 in 3 chance to give X2.5 mult for the rest of the round every round.

Letdown

X4 mult on final hand if 80% of required chips are already scored.

The Spritesheet

Gains +15 chips every time a Joker is bought this run.

Stop Sign

$2 for every unused discard at end of round.

Melody

X4 chips if played hand has exactly 4 cards.

Flipnote Joker

Gains X0.1 mult for every consecutive played hand with a Diamond card.

Typogram

Gives the amount of letters in the leftmost Joker's name as mult.

Discounted Joker

Sell this Joker for a free Coupon Tag.

Anonymous Joker

Wild and Stone cards give X1.5 mult.

Miner

If first hand played is a single Stone Card, 1 in 2 chance to give $25, destroys card

Splotch

$4 for every wild non-face card in deck at end of round.

Flushpilled Joker

Gains X0.25 mult per consecutive flush played.

Manga Joker

Creates a random Tarot card if played hand only contains Spade cards. (High Card excluded)

Pen Doodle

Retriggers all played cards if played hand only contains Club cards.

Love Letter

Played Heart cards have a 1 in 2 chance to give X2 mult or not score at all.

Highlighted Joker

X0.1 mult for every enhanced Diamond card in deck.

Goobertito

This Joker gains +25 chips for every 15 Heart or Diamond cards scored.
